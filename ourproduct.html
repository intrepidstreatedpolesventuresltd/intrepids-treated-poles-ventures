FENCING POLES

Versatile Poles For Every Fencing Need

  • Our fencing poles are designed to offer long-lasting solutions for farms, homes, institutions, and commercial spaces.
  • Available in lengths of 6 to 9 feet and diameters ranging from 3 to 9 inches, these poles are perfect for boundary fencing, livestock enclosures, and general security.
  • Their sturdy build and uniform size make them easy to work with during installation.

Longevity Engineered With CCA Treatment

  • We offer both treated and untreated poles. Treated poles undergo a professional preservation process using Copper Chromium Arsenic (CCA), which enhances their resistance to termites, rot, and extreme weather. With this treatment, the poles can last up to 30 years when properly installed and maintained.
Fair Prices For Long-Lasting Fencing Solutions
  • Retail pricing varies based on size and treatment option, with bulk purchase discounts available.
  • We also offer nationwide delivery and professional installation services on request, ensuring your fencing project is handled from start to finish with efficiency and quality.

UTILITY POLES

Utility Poles for All Purposes

  • Our utility poles are engineered for strength, safety, and multi-purpose use.
  • They are ideal for electricity distribution, telecommunication lines (including fiber), and construction support such as scaffolding or form work.
  • With their durability and versatility, they are a go-to solution for infrastructure and industrial projects.

Sizes to Match Every Project

  • We supply poles in a wide range of sizes to suit different utility needs. Lengths range from 4 to 46 feet, and diameters from 2 to 9 inches. Whether you’re installing short-distance site poles or long-span transmission lines, we have the right size for your job.
Treated for Long-Term Performance
  • Our treated utility poles undergo Copper Chromium Arsenic (CCA) preservation — a trusted process that protects wood from termites, decay, and harsh weather. Treated poles can last for up to 30 years, making them a smart, long-term investment for both temporary and permanent installations.

Retail and Bulk Pricing

  • We offer fair, competitive pricing on all our utility poles, with costs based on size and treatment type. Bulk purchase discounts are available for large projects or repeat buyers. We’re committed to giving you top value without compromising on quality.

Delivery Anywhere in Kenya

  • Need poles on-site fast? We provide reliable nationwide delivery to ensure your project stays on track. Whether you're ordering a few poles or a full truckload, we handle the logistics so you can focus on the build.
