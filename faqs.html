2.What type of trees do you harvest for your products?
We primarily harvest mature Eucalyptus Grandis /Saligna trees, commonly referred to in Kenya as Eucalyptus Blue Gum, aged between 7 to 10 years. This species is ideal for CCA (Copper Chrome Arsenic) treatment, offering excellent strength and durability for fencing and utility pole use.
3.Do you source your own trees or buy from local farmers?
Yes, we do. We have our own fully managed plantation located in Lamu, which allows us to maintain high standards of quality and consistency. In addition, we source trees from selected partner farms that meet our quality and sustainability standards. This ensures reliable supply and supports our environmental commitment.
4. Do you provide fencing services?
Yes, we provide fencing services. Our team is equipped to handle both small and large fencing projects professionally and efficiently. For more details, feel free to contact us directly.
5.Do you offer transport services?
Yes, we do. Delivery is free if your location is more than 100 km from our yard. For shorter distances, delivery terms can be discussed when placing your order.
6.Do you deliver outside your operational areas?
Yes, we deliver to all towns and regions across Kenya, including remote areas. Delivery logistics are arranged after order confirmation.
7.How long does it take to process an order?
Most orders are fulfilled within three (3) working days. We keep a ready stock of seasoned poles, ensuring fast treatment and dispatch after your order is placed.
8.Can I have my poles drilled?
Yes, we offer custom pole drilling services based on your specifications. The cost depends on the size and thickness of the pole.
9. Do you offer discounts?
Yes, we offer discounts based on quantity and other specific order details.
10. Is there a warranty on your poles?
Yes, our treated poles come with an average warranty of 30 years, depending on usage and environmental exposure. All products are KEBS-certified and meet industry standards.
11. Do you sell other related products?
Yes, we also sell affordable firewood, a by-product of our timber processing, suitable for home and industrial use.
12. How can I place an order?
You can order by calling ,emailing us ,or visiting our yard .We also process orders through WhatsApp for faster communication .
13. How long do treated poles last ?
With proper treatment and maintenance ,our poles can last 15 to 25 years depending on environmental conditions .
14.Can I get a quotation before placing an order?
Absolutely. You can contact us for a free quotation based on your specific project requirements (But a mandatory site visit must be done first for accurate figures )
